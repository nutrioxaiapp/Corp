Account Deletion – NutrioX AI

NutrioX AI respects your right to control your personal data. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email from your registered email address to:
    nutrioxai.app@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line:
    Account Deletion Request
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your request to permanently delete your NutrioX AI account

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon verification, we will permanently delete:

  • Your user account
  • Email address associated with the account
  • User-generated content (food images, nutrition inputs)
  • Stored preferences and settings

What Data May Be Retained?

Certain non-identifiable or legally required data may be retained for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security and audit purposes

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 7–30 days, depending on verification and legal requirements.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, please contact:
nutrioxai.app@gmail.com
